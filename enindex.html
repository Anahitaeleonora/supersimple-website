Anahita is an artist of Persian and Norwegian heritage, raised in Alver on Norway’s west coast. Her paintings capture intimate, close encounters between people, exploring the body, gaze, and human connection. Using subtle, earthy tones, her work draws inspiration from Cubism and invites viewers to find their own meaning in each piece.
