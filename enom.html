Anahita is a 24-year-old artist who has always expressed herself through various forms of art. Nevertheless, she chose a more theoretical path and began studying engineering, believing it would be difficult to make a living from her creativity. While she met close friends during her studies, she lacked a space to fully explore her artistic side. Midway through her degree, she took a year off to travel through Latin America. The experience, particularly her time in Mexico, left a profound mark on both her inner life and her creativity, giving her the curiosity and courage to direct her art.
